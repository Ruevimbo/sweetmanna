About Sweet Manna

Welcome to Sweet Manna. Sweet Manna is more than just a bakery — it’s a place where joy is baked into every bite. Founded by Ruvimbo Muchaonyerwa, Sweet Manna was created to share comfort, love, and faith through the simple delight of sweet treats.

At Sweet Manna, we believe baking is both an art and a ministry. Every cake, pastry, and cookie carries not only the flavors of quality ingredients but also the values of faith, love, and community. Whether it’s a family celebration, a small gathering, or just a personal craving, our baked goods are meant to nourish the body and uplift the soul.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to bring people joy through freshly baked treats that are crafted with love, quality ingredients, and a passion for flavor. We aim to create a space where every customer feels at home, every order is handled with care, and every bite is a reminder of life’s sweet blessings.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to become the community’s favorite bakery — a place where families, friends, and neighbors come together to share in the joy of good food. We seek to be recognized for creativity in flavors, excellence in service, and a commitment to spreading happiness, faith, and togetherness through everything we serve.

Core Values

At Sweet Manna, our values guide everything we do:

  • Faith – Trusting God as the source of creativity and provision.
  • Love – Putting care into every recipe and every customer interaction.
  • Excellence – Using the best ingredients and maintaining high standards.
  • Community – Creating treats that bring people together.
  • Joy – Ensuring that every product leaves a smile on someone’s face.

Our Story

The name “Sweet Manna” comes from the Bible story of manna — the bread from heaven that fed God’s people. Just like that gift, our bakery exists to bring daily comfort and delight to everyone who walks through our doors. What began as a small dream has grown into a space where faith meets flavor, and where every recipe carries a piece of the founder’s heart.
